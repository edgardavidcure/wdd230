WHO WE ARE

Bountiful Foods is a locally-owned and operated company based in Carlsbad, California that specializes in producing high-quality, 100% organic fruit bags and fruit drinks. The company was founded in 2010 by Jane Smith, who wanted to provide the community with fresh, healthy, and locally-sourced produce. With a passion for sustainability and organic farming practices, Jane started Bountiful Foods with the goal of providing delicious and nutritious fruits that support the local economy.

OUR MISSION

The company's mission is to promote healthy living through the production and distribution of organic fruit bags and fruit drinks that are locally sourced and environmentally sustainable. Bountiful Foods believes that the health of our community is directly linked to the quality of the food we consume, and they strive to provide their customers with the best possible products. They work closely with local farmers and suppliers to ensure that their products are always fresh and of the highest quality, while minimizing their impact on the environment.

OUR COMMITMENT

At Bountiful Foods, we are committed to creating a sustainable business model that supports the local community. They prioritize fair and ethical practices throughout their supply chain, from sourcing to distribution. Additionally, they donate a portion of their profits to local charities and organizations that support community health and wellness initiatives. With a focus on quality, sustainability, and community, Bountiful Foods is dedicated to providing delicious, healthy, and locally-sourced fruits to the people of Carlsbad and beyond.
