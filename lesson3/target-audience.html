Home Design Target Audience

Montclair Chamber Of Commerce

Audience Description

The audience we want to reach, is people who are looking for places to shop, visit, explore, and feel welcomed in the city of Montclair. We also want to catch businesses attention to our website, where they will be able to explore a catalog of all the businesses in the community, and use that information to explore areas of opportunity like advertising, partnering, and/or gaining new customers.

Persona #1

John Smith, male, 50 years old, has lived in Montclair for more than 30 years, and has seen lots of new bussinesses open in the area, but is not familiar with the services they offer. He does not want to visit a store in person and feel pressured by sales people to get their services, rather he wants to explore the different options, and learn more about them from the commodity of his home. John owns a computer and will be accessing the website from home, however, he does not like ads and is exceptical to visit websites that are not well organized and have a lot of useless ads.

Persona #2

A local Bussiness who recently started operations as a computer sales and repair store, called TechyTech. The bussiness owners are 2 brothers who have worked in IT for more than 10 years, whose names are John Doe and Joseph Doe. They are interested in gaining more customers and reach more people in the community, without expending lots of money on advertisement. They are very familiar with websites and want to use the chamber of commerce website to advertise their sales promotions and repair catalog. By the end of the year 2023 they want to be well-stablished in the community as one of the main stores for computer sales and repairs. They will access the site to get to know their competitors without having to visit their store in person, and use the information to improve their services and offers. They will access their website from their work Tablets and Computers.

Scenario #1

Daniel, a homeowner, has been suffering from water leaking in his main bathroom, he wants to get in touch with a good plumber who can fix it. There are independent plumbers and plumbing companies in the city, however, he wants to get a quote from each of them without having to travel around the city trying to find out where they are located and spend gas.

Scenario #2

A 60 year old man who is retired, wants to start playing tennis and join a club. He does not know anybody who plays tennis, but he heard that there is a tennis club in Montclair. He wants to join a membership and get in touch with the club members to play every week. However, he does not know where the tennis club is located in the city, and he wants to know more about it while he is enjoying his retirement from home. He only has a cellphone and a tablet.

Scenario #3

A local bussiness has recently started to make public events where they intend to advertise their products. They are a beverage company and want to invite people from the city to hike in the mountains, and sell their drinks. They want to increase their sales for the year and gain new customers while stablishing a bond with the people. They want to advertise their event through the chamber of commerce website.
