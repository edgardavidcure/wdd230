Site Purpose

This site intends to help locals to gain further knowledge of the businesses available in the city of montclair, along with activities, attractions and places to visit in the area. Furhtermore, this site intends to help local businesses advertise their products and offers, to help them reach more customers who might benefit from their services. On our side, we intend to provide all of our users with a functional website that provides clear information, that is well organized, and provide contact information for all those who would like to get in touch.
